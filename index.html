About Our Games

Our Mission

We create accessible educational games that help children with disabilities learn and grow in a fun, supportive environment.

New: Face Recognition Technology

Our Emotion Faces Match game now includes advanced face recognition technology! Children can use their own facial expressions to play, making the learning experience more interactive and engaging.

Accessibility Features

  • Large, easy-to-see buttons and text
  • Clear audio feedback and instructions
  • Simple, intuitive controls
  • High contrast color options
  • Keyboard and touch navigation
  • Reduced motion support
  • Face recognition for interactive play

Educational Benefits

  • Improves cognitive skills
  • Enhances motor coordination
  • Builds confidence and self-esteem
  • Develops social-emotional skills
  • Encourages independent learning
  • Teaches emotion recognition through face recognition

Help & Support

Face Recognition Setup

To use face recognition features:

  1. Allow camera access when prompted
  2. Ensure good lighting on your face
  3. Position yourself in front of the camera
  4. Make clear facial expressions

How to Play

Each game has simple instructions and is designed to be easy to understand and play.

Accessibility Options

Our games automatically adapt to your device's accessibility settings, including high contrast mode and reduced motion preferences.

Getting Started

  1. Choose a game from the Games section
  2. Read the instructions carefully
  3. Start playing and have fun!
  4. Take breaks when needed

Privacy & Security

Face recognition runs entirely in your browser. No video or images are sent to our servers. Your privacy is protected.

If you need help or have suggestions for improving our games, please contact us.
